# Clarifying Questions — AI Study Companion for JEE

> _Answer as many as you can in plain English. Skip any that don't apply. The sharper your answers, the better the research, PRD, and plan that follow. Back answers with specifics (names, times, numbers) where possible — vague answers produce vague plans._

**Idea brief (as captured):** An AI-first study app for Indian students preparing for JEE (Main + Advanced). ~15 lakh students register every year, most prep for 2 years, incumbents (BYJU's, Unacademy, Vedantu, PhysicsWallah, Allen) are expensive, coaching-class-heavy, and built around video lectures rather than personalized 1:1 AI tutoring. Goal: find the wedge, sharpen the slice, and design a product a solo founder could start building in the next 1–2 weeks.

**Tier assessment:** **T2 / S2** — Moderate-complexity consumer SaaS/mobile app in a crowded vertical with named incumbents, solo-founder builder who's shipped code before and knows JTBD/PRD vocabulary. The category is big enough to need rigor but the MVP can be narrow enough to avoid T3 heavy-weights (Porter, PESTEL).

**Why this tier:** Multi-user consumer product with a real ARPU conversation, named competitors, and a "why now" AI unlock — but the founder is solo, not raising a Series A, and doesn't need a full regulatory/multi-year roadmap.

## 1. Who and Pain

> _Who this is for and why it hurts. If we get this wrong, nothing downstream is worth the time._

1. **Who, exactly, is the beachhead student?** Pick one: (a) Class 11 student just starting the 2-year JEE grind, (b) Class 12 student in their final push, (c) "droppers" — students taking a gap year after Class 12 to retry JEE, (d) tier-2/tier-3 city students who can't afford Kota-level coaching, (e) Kota-enrolled students using this as a supplement. Each has different pain, wallet, and distribution.
2. **Describe the last time a student in your target segment hit the pain this solves.** Walk through one concrete evening: what chapter, what happened when they got stuck, what they did (Googled? YouTube? Asked a friend? Gave up?), what did that cost them (time, confidence, a night of sleep)?
3. **Pain × frequency × urgency:** How painful is "I'm stuck on a problem at 10pm and nobody's available to help" — mild annoyance, or the thing that breaks a 2-year plan? How often does it happen — daily, weekly? How urgent — needs solving now, or can wait till class tomorrow?
4. **Who already has a trusted relationship with this student and is *not* solving this problem well?** (Signal: your distribution partners. E.g., school teachers, tutors, YouTube creators who teach JEE, existing coaching classes, Telegram groups.)

## 2. Scope and Wedge

> _What's the thin slice you're going after first, and why it's the wedge._

5. **Of these five candidate JTBDs, pick ONE as the MVP target (rank the rest):**
   - (a) "Help me solve a stuck problem right now with step-by-step reasoning" (doubt-solving)
   - (b) "Tell me what to study today given my weak chapters and the test date" (personalized plan)
   - (c) "Explain this concept at my level until I actually get it" (AI concept tutor / Socratic dialogue)
   - (d) "Grade my mock tests and show me my real weak spots" (analytics + test-series)
   - (e) "Keep me motivated and not broken" (emotional/coach layer)
6. **What does "magic day 1" look like?** Write the single moment where a JEE student says "holy shit" — 2 sentences.
7. **What's the 10x angle?** Price (5x cheaper than Allen/PW)? Speed (instant 24×7 vs waiting for a teacher)? Quality (Socratic teaching beats video lectures)? Personalization (adapts to THIS student's gaps)? Access (works on a ₹8k Android, poor connection)?
8. **Are we English-first, Hinglish-first, or multilingual (Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi) from day 1?** (This is a huge scope decision — tier-2/3 students need Hinglish; Kota students can handle English.)

## 3. Constraints and Goals

> _What you're building within, and what success looks like._

9. **Time + money budget:** Full-time founder push or nights/weekends? How many months of runway before this has to show revenue or stop? Any co-founder or is this genuinely solo?
10. **Monetization hypothesis:** Freemium with a ₹199–499/mo paid tier? One-time unlock? Free with school/coaching partnership deals? (JEE families spend ₹1–2 lakh/yr on coaching — how much will they pay you?)
11. **What's the 3-month and 12-month success picture?** Specific: "500 DAU in Hindi tier-2/3 beta by month 3; ₹5L MRR by month 12" — not "we'll see."
12. **Non-goals you're already sure of?** (E.g., "Will NOT record video lectures." "Will NOT replace coaching classes." "Will NOT build a marketplace of human tutors." Non-goals are diamonds.)
